KIM TAEHYUNG

Kim Tae-hyung born 30 December 1995 known professionally as V , is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and a member of the boy band BTS.Since his debut with the band in 2013, V has performed three solo songs under their name—"Stigma" in 2016, "Singularity" in 2018, and "Inner Child" in 2020—all of which charted on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart.

Early Life and Education

V first aspired to be a professional singer in elementary school.With his father's support,he began taking saxophone lessons saxophone lessons in early middle school as a means of pursuing a musical career.V eventually became a trainee for Big Hit Entertainment after passing an audition in Daegu.

After graduating from Korean Arts High School in 2014, V enrolled in Global Cyber University, graduating in August 2020 with a major in Broadcasting and Entertainment.As of 2021, he is enrolled at Hanyang Cyber University, pursuing a Master of Business Administration in Advertising and Media.

Personal Life

Since 2018, V has lived in Hannam-dong, Seoul, South Korea with his bandmates.[79] In July 2019, he bought an apartment worth ₩5.1 billion (2019) (US$4.38 million).
